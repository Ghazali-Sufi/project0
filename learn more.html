Our Purpose

Our purpose is to develop in our students the knowledge, skills and
qualities, strengthening their character and resolve, so that they are
well qualified and prepared for their future success and wellbeing.

Our Values

Quality, Trust, Self-motivation, Innovation, Hands-on,
Learning & Teaching, and Equanimity.

Our aims

We are a Academy where students:

  • have ambition, work hard and make excellent progress.
  • believe that by applying themselves they can get better.
  • are involved in their own learning and help drive school decisions.
  • have pride in their school and have high expectations for themselves and their peers.
